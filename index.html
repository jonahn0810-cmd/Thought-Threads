Welcome to Thought Threads

Thought Threads is a student-led program that connects students with seniors in retirement homes through safe, reviewed, handwritten letters. Our mission is to reduce loneliness, stimulate cognitive engagement, and foster meaningful intergenerational bonds.

Explore the pages above to learn how the program works, our safety measures, and how you can get involved.
